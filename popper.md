# Karl Popper

Wrote of falsification, which says that for a theory to be science, it needs to be falsifiable. Namely, it must be possible to refute. Einstein general relativity an example of this.
Prominent critique of [[Marx]], linear mode of tevelopment as tautology.
Related to [[Kuhn]] in different conceptions of scientific endeavour. Kuhn thought building blocs and paradigms. Popper thought scientific endeavour about creative critique of existing theory.
